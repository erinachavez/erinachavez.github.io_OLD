Hello! I am a current sophomore attending the Gallatin School of Individualized Study at New York University. There, I am combining graphic arts, web design, and computer programming to create an all encompassing major that serves my future goals to be a creative and skilled web developer. My experience ranges from small projects I've done to help on-campus clubs, to being hired to redesign websites for small businesses.

My interest in web design began early in high school, when we were expected to maintain a digital portfolio of the projects and assignments we had completed. Being born in Koshigaya, Japan and raised in San Diego, California my design aesthetics combined both Japan's unyielding attention to detail, and California's adventurous warmth. Now, living and studying in New York, I also strive to ensure that my work is focused, minimal, and effective.

Outside of my school and work pursuits, I enjoy playing guitar, playing muggle quidditch, traveling, and tutoring those interested in the computer sciences.
