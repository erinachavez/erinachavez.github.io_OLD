A Bach flower remedies consultation is an informal discussion via phone or email with the goal of finding a remedy combination that suits your current situation.

Some Things We Will Cover in the First Consultation:

  • Answering your questions about what the remedies contain, how they are made, their history, and safety
  • Addressing your current situation and phase in life, feelings, patterns, or habits that you would like to work on by taking remedies, and suggest remedies for you to try
  • Instructions on how to take the remedies
  • Where you can learn more about the system and how to use it
  • Suggest a time frame for a follow-up consultation if desired, usually 2-3 weeks from the first consultation
